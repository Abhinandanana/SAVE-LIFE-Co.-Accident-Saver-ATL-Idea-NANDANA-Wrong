PROBLEM

Problem Statement

Our Idea- Accident Saver, is for preventing road accidents which take place because of the following reasons-

1. Some drivers don’t realize the distance between their car and the car in front of them as they are lost in their own thoughts, or they are talking on their mobile phone while driving.

2. Sometimes there is a foggy weather and they can’t see the car in front of them due to low visibility. Due to the above reasons, driver could accidently bump into the car in front of them which is dangerous for the passengers and drivers of both the cars.



SOLUTION

Solution

So, to reduce the number of accidents caused by the given problem, Save Life Co. has an idea- Accident Saver,-

1. If we have a sensor built in cars and the sensor is coded/programmed in such a way so that whenever the distance between 2 cars is 2 metres or less than 2 metres, the sensor will start beeping loudly.

2. By hearing the beeping sound, the driver will get aware about the distance between his/her car and the car ahead. To prevent the accident the driver will immediately increase the distance between his/her car and the car ahead by reducing the speed or by putting break.

Thus, the sensor named Accident Saver by Save Life Co., will prevent cars bumping into each other, accidents on highways and save precious lives.
